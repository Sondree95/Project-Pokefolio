# Why We Exist

Collecting should be exciting.

Too often, it isn't.

Collectors spend countless hours comparing prices across marketplaces, researching products, watching YouTube videos, scrolling through Reddit, and asking the same questions over and over again.

> "Is this a good price?"

> "Am I paying too much?"

> "Should I buy this now or wait?"

Experienced collectors eventually learn to answer these questions.

New collectors often cannot.

Project Pokéfolio exists to remove uncertainty and replace it with confidence.

We believe every collector deserves access to trustworthy data, transparent analysis, and tools that make collecting easier—not more complicated.

---

# Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted decision platform for collectors.

We envision a future where every collector—whether buying their very first Elite Trainer Box or managing a collection worth millions—can make confident decisions backed by reliable information.

Pokémon Trading Card Game is our starting point.

It is not our destination.

Project Pokéfolio is being built as a scalable platform capable of supporting many collectible markets while staying true to the same core philosophy.

---

# What We Believe

We believe that:

- Trust is earned through transparency.
- Good decisions come from good data.
- Simplicity is harder to build than complexity.
- Every collector deserves professional tools.
- Technology should reduce uncertainty—not create it.

---

# What Success Looks Like

Project Pokéfolio succeeds when collectors:

- Open Pokéfolio before making a purchase.
- Trust our recommendations.
- Spend less time researching.
- Feel more confident.
- Build better collections through better decisions.

Success is not measured by downloads.

Success is measured by trust.

---

# Long-Term Ambition

Project Pokéfolio aims to become the leading decision platform for collectible assets.

Although Pokémon Trading Card Game is our first category, the platform is designed to expand into additional collectible markets without changing its core mission.

Every new category should benefit from the same principles:

- Trusted market data
- Transparent decision support
- Portfolio management
- Market intelligence
- Exceptional user experience

---

# Guiding Principle

Every feature we build must answer one question:

**Does this help collectors make smarter decisions?**

If the answer is no, we should rethink it.
