# Core Problem

Collectors often struggle to determine whether a collectible product is worth buying.

Finding accurate market information requires searching across multiple websites, comparing prices, evaluating trends and relying on personal experience.

This process is time-consuming, confusing and creates unnecessary uncertainty—especially for new collectors.

Project Pokéfolio exists to help collectors make confident purchasing decisions.

# Core User

Our primary users are collectors who want confidence before making a purchase.

They may be buying their first Elite Trainer Box or expanding an existing collection.

Regardless of experience, they all share the same need:

They want to know whether they are making a good buying decision.

# Core Value Proposition

Pokéfolio gives collectors the confidence to buy smarter by turning complex market data into simple, trustworthy decisions.

# Product Positioning

Pokéfolio is a collector decision platform.

Our purpose is not simply to display prices or track collections.

Our purpose is to help collectors make confident decisions throughout their collecting journey.

Everything else exists to support that mission.

Pokéfolio is not:

- A marketplace
- A Pokédex
- A discussion forum
- A social media platform
- A simple price database

Pokéfolio is:

- A collector decision platform
- A trusted source of market intelligence
- A portfolio companion
- A decision support tool

# North Star Metric

Time to Decision

Goal:

Enable collectors to make confident buying decisions in under 10 seconds.

# Product Principles

- Trust over hype
- Start with the user's question
- Reduce research
- Increase confidence
- Simplicity over complexity
- Explain every recommendation
- Mobile first

# MVP Definition

The first version of Pokéfolio should enable collectors to:

- Search for a product
- View current market price
- Compare asking price to market value
- Receive a buying recommendation
- Understand why that recommendation was made

# Success Criteria

The MVP is successful when a collector can:

- Search for a product in seconds
- Understand the current market price
- Decide whether to buy the product
- Feel confident in that decision

# Open Questions

- Which market data sources should be supported?
- How should recommendations be calculated?
- How should confidence scores work?
- Should barcode scanning be included in the MVP?

# Strategic Decisions

The following strategic decisions define the direction of Project Pokéfolio.

## SD-001

**Pokéfolio is a collector decision platform.**

Collectors do not need more data.

They need confidence to make better decisions.

Every feature developed for Pokéfolio should help users make faster, smarter and more confident collecting decisions.

---

## SD-002

**Start with the user's question.**

The first experience should begin with the collector's goal—not with our technology.

Instead of asking users to search for a product, Pokéfolio should help answer the question:

> **"Should I buy this?"**

Every workflow should begin with the user's decision.

---

## SD-003

**Reduce research. Increase confidence.**

Collectors should not have to compare multiple marketplaces, spreadsheets, forums and videos before making a purchase.

Pokéfolio exists to reduce research time while increasing confidence in every decision.

---

## SD-004

**Trust is our competitive advantage.**

Users should understand why every recommendation is made.

Recommendations must always be transparent, explainable and supported by data.
